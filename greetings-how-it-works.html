How it works

How to create Greetings and Invitation

After you login follow these easy steps:

Step 1: Choose theme

Choose a template from one of our attractive themes.

Step 2: Customization and payment

Provide the details for your card and make payment.
• Within minutes you will receive a link to your customized video.
• You can either accept the video or request edits (maximum two times).

Step 3: Personalization and final video

You can personalize the card by providing names of the recipients.
• You can copy your premade list, or add names directly, in our spread sheet.
• After personalization payment (if pending), videos will be available for download shortly.

Note:

• As we upload the videos we will email you instructions to download your videos.
• All videos will be deleted from our server after one month, please make sure to collect all videos before that.
