About Us


We offer break-fast, lunch and supper in a day and made this service flexible that anyone can order food for a particular day or particular meal separately by looking at our ‘Today’s menu’ online, apart from monthly packages.
We have a chain of flexible team members who extend their services in all regions in and out of Jodhpur. These include Mohali, Panchkulla, Zirakpur as of now. We ensure the quick delivery of food at your place as soon as you place the order. We keep a track on spiciness of our food items without compromising the taste so that one can prefer it on long term basis without any health issues that are commonly related to outside food.
